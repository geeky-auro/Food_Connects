  • What are the requirements to become a Chef (seller) at Food Connects?
    1. own a home-scale food or beverage business (not a restaurant).
    2. produce your own products (not resellers & factories).
    3. producing food & drinks that are easy to deliver by motorbike and do not require very special handling, such as cold temperatures, delivered very slowly or carefully.
    4. has a Jabodetabek production/kitchen area (for now, due to our limited capacity, we are prioritizing the Jabodetabek area).
    5. have an email that is always active to receive notifications (for this phase we provide notifications via email. Applications for Google Play and App Store are in progress).
    6. have a BCA account (to transfer sales proceeds).
    7. have signed up at backtokimi.com
  • How do you operate Food Connects?
    Account

    Account is your account with Food Connects. You can see your income from sales, as well as the weekly cash out that you have to pay into your account every week.

    Channel

    Channel is your place to sell.
    You can create several channels if you have several food stores.
    Your shops can also be managed by several users who you give authority to manage.
    Or if you just want to cook and monitor, choose a friend or relative or child who you trust can help you with the transaction.

    Channel Details : here you can see your shop with your special products.

    Edit Channel: fill in information about your Channel (shop) such as Channel name, Production Address (kitchen), mobile phone number that can contacted, as well as a description of your shop. Make sure your channel status is active so buyers can find your channel.

    Image Management : this is like a banner for your Channel/shop. Upload a logo, image of a best seller product or a product that you want to increase sales of or an announcement about a new product from your channel. Choose File, then select the image you want to upload.

    Order Dashboard: all incoming orders can be checked here. History of orders and orders that are currently in the request & payment pending process.

    Product Dashboard : upload your products here.

    • Create New Product: click every time you want to create a new product. Give the name, price per unit, minimum order, size per unit (box/bottle/jar/jar/bowl/etc.), how long it takes to make the product, food information, and type of wet or dry product.
    • Image Management: upload product images according to the information provided.
    • Product Statistics: your parameters to find out who has seen your product, how many, how many times, so you can specifically approach the Buyer.
    Edit Profile

    Edit your profile, of course, to change your name and include your birthday.

    Change Password

    You can change your password here.

  • Is there a fee if you join as a Chef at Food Connects?

    backtokimi.com charges 10% on every successful transaction.
    Example:
    Buyer orders…
    Green Crunchy Brownies (20 x 10 x 5cm) = 10 pcs
    Brownies with matcha taste & aroma, with a crispy rice crunch sensation.
    Rp. 75,000 (without delivery price)

    Buyer's address: Karet, Semanggi, South Jakarta
    for example delivery fee = Rp. 25,000 (delivery costs will be calculated by our system)
    So, what the Buyer must pay:
    Rp. 75,000 x 10 = Rp. 750,000 + Rp. 25,000 (postage)
    Total: Rp. 775,000
    So, the money you will receive from this transaction is Rs. 675,000.
    You can increase product prices or increase the minimum order to remain profitable.

  • What happens if a Buyer orders a product?
    • On your Order Dashboard there will be an Order Request and you will receive a notification via your email that you have registered with backtokimi.com.
    • check the date & time the Buyer wants in the Request Delivery Time.
      • If you feel unable, you can submit a date or time that you feel able to carry out the order. Buyer will be notified. Buyer can accept a "Request", change the date, or "Cancel" the proposed date or time that you have submitted.
      • If you feel capable, you can immediately “Approve” the Order Request.
    • After that, the Buyer will be notified to make payment.
    • Our system will check the payment made by the Buyer and notify you on your Order Dashboard, that Pending Transactions have become Paid Transactions, and you can work on the order according to the date and hours that have been submitted.
    • Food Connects will immediately schedule a server (delivery) from the delivery service to pick up & deliver your order on the agreed date and time.
  • What is the meaning of Pre Order?

    Orders ordered today (max. 14.00), will be received the next day at the earliest at 09.30 and at the latest at 18.30. If after 14.00, the order will be made & delivered the next day. If you feel that this time is still not enough, you can immediately change the Request Delivery Time for the order entered on the Order Dashboard.

  • What is Pick Up Time and Delivery Time?

    Pick Up Time is the time/hour when the Server (deliverer) arrives at the Chef's kitchen location. Delivery Time is the time/hour when the order arrives at the Buyer's location where it has been ordered.

  • What is Special Instruction?

    Instructions that must be paid attention to by the Chef regarding allergies that Buyers have or their dislikes or dietary concerns regarding the orders they order

  • How does the money paid by the Buyer flow to the Chef's account?
    • Every Buyer who orders via backtokimi.com will transfer their money for the total cost of the order and shipping to the company's BCA (MAYA KATALIS CIPTA BUANA) account first.
    • Food Connects will add up all transactions that occurred in 1 week (Monday to Saturday), and we will transfer every Wednesday of the following week for all transactions that occurred 1 week ago.

      Example:
      First week of transactions

      Monday, January 1 2017
      Rp. 500,000 (5 transactions)

      Tuesday, January 2 2017
      Rp. 700,000 (7 transactions)

      Wednesday, January 3 2017
      Rp. 800,000 (8 transactions)

      Thursday, January 4 2017
      Rp. 500,000 (5 transactions)

      Friday, January 5 2017
      Rp. 500,000 (5 transactions)

      Total: Rp. 3,000,000 (30 transactions)
      So we will transfer Rp. 2,700,000 (10% deducted) on Wednesday, January 10 2017 to your account.

  • What if the Buyer cancels the order?

    If the Buyer has paid for the order, the Buyer cannot cancel the order.

  • What if the server (delivery) does not arrive according to the order schedule?

    If the server does not arrive within 15 minutes, please report it immediately to alyssa.prabandari@backtokimi.com

  • What if the Chef is unplannedly unable to carry out the orders that have been ordered?

    Food Connects will return the Buyer's money and Chef must pay the consequences of this incident.

  • Can the Chef personally deliver orders that come in via Food Connects?

    No. So that we can still track couriers (deliverers), because we have collaborated with several delivery services that have a system to find out the whereabouts of servers (deliverers). So, both buyers, chefs and chefs can be clearly informed about the status of orders being delivered.
